How to Use the Real-time Chat

The real-time chat feature allows users to interact with experts instantly. To use the chat:

  1. Click on the "Real-time Chat" link in the navigation menu.
  2. Type your question or message in the input field.
  3. Click the "Send" button to submit your message.
  4. Engage in a conversation with the expert who will provide real-time responses.
